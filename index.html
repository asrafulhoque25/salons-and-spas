The real problems

8 reasons most salon and spa websites quietly hand bookings to Vagaro

Service menu buried three taps deep

The average salon visitor decides within 14 seconds whether your site is worth their time. If your nail extension price isn't visible without tapping 'Services → Nails → Extensions,' you've already lost to the salon whose Google listing showed pricing right in the snippet.

Booking widget is a third-party iframe

Most salons hand booking to Vagaro or Booksy and embed a 600px iframe that breaks mobile and dilutes branding. The booking widget should feel native to your site, not like an ad for the scheduling platform you pay $30 a month to use.

No transparent pricing — and prospects bounce to find it

'Call for pricing' is dead. Beauty consumers expect to see at least a starting price before they invest emotional energy in choosing your salon. Without it, you lose them to the salon that publishes a menu with ranges.

Stylist portfolios live on Instagram, not your site

Your best work is on someone else's platform earning them ad revenue. Google can't index Instagram captions reliably, so a search for 'balayage Phoenix' will never surface that photo your stylist tagged six months ago.

One 'Services' page where you need eight

If 'Balayage,' 'Brazilian Blowout,' and 'Color Correction' share one URL, none of them rank. Each service is a separate search intent and deserves a dedicated page targeting that specific keyword.

Photo galleries take 8 seconds to load on mobile

Beauty is a visual category, but unoptimized galleries kill mobile conversion. Every extra second of load time loses you another 7% of the visitors you paid (in time and SEO) to attract.

No reviews showing on the site itself

You have 4.9 stars on Google, but your homepage shows zero reviews. Visitors won't open a new tab to check Yelp — they'll just leave. Reviews need to live where the decision is being made.

No gift card purchase flow

Gift cards are 18 to 28 percent of December salon revenue at well-run shops. Sites without an online gift card flow send those buyers to your competitor whose 'Buy a Gift Card' button is in the header year-round.
